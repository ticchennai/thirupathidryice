How long does dry ice last?

Dry ice sublimates at roughly 5–10% of its mass per day in a standard cooler box, and slower in an insulated container. For longer trips, order slightly more than you need and keep it in a well-sealed, insulated box.

Is dry ice safe to handle?

Dry ice is safe when handled correctly. Always use gloves or tongs since it can cause cold burns on bare skin, store it in a ventilated area (never in a sealed container, as it expands), and keep it out of reach of children.
